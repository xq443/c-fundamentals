bubble sort:
	time complexity:
		worst case: O(n^2)
		best case: O(n)
		average case :O(n^2)
	space complexity: O(1)

merge sort:
	time complexity:
		worse case: O(nlogn)
	space complexity:
		O(n)
quick sort:
	time complexity:
		worst case: O(nlogn)
	space complexity:
		O(logn)

selection sort:
	time complexity:
		worse case: O(n^2)
	space complexity:
		O(1)
insertion sort:
	time complexity:
		worst case O(n^2)
	space complexity:
		O(1)
